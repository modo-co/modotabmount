## Tablet Charging Control

Automatically turns tablet charging **ON** when battery is low and **OFF** when
battery is high. Designed for wall-mounted tablets and smart chargers.

**Default behavior**
- Charging ON at **≤ 20%**
- Charging OFF at **≥ 80%**
